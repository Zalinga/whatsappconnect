Ad Placement Guide
Three distinct zones — each optimised for different goals and budgets.
📢
Leaderboard Banner
728 × 90 px · Above the fold
The highest-visibility slot on the portal. Displayed above the main content area, seen by every user on every page load. Best for brand awareness campaigns and product launches.
Highest CTR Brand Awareness 100% Viewability
📦
Sidebar Banner
250 × 120 px · Sticky left column
Sticky placement in the left sidebar, visible throughout the user's entire session as they type and connect. Ideal for local businesses, service providers, and app promotions.
Session-long Local Business Low Cost
📋
In-Table Native Ad
Full width row · Contacts table
A native-style sponsored row inserted every 5 contacts in the table. Blends naturally with content, extremely low banner-blindness. Perfect for SaaS tools, B2B services, and fintech.
Native Feel B2B Targeting High Engagement
🎯
Sponsored Review
Review card · Reviews tab
A clearly labelled sponsored card in the Reviews section. Shown to users who are actively evaluating the portal — high intent, high trust context. Great for competing products or complementary SaaS tools.
High Intent Trust Context SaaS / Apps
